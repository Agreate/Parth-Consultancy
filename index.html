Our Services

We provide end-to-end accounting services that are tailored for small and medium businesses. Choose a package or request a custom plan.

Bookkeeping & Ledger Management

Daily / monthly recording of transactions, chart of accounts setup, trial balance and ledger maintenance.

  • Daybook and journal entries
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Expense categorization

GST & Compliance

GST return preparation, GSTR filing assistance, reconciliation of Input Tax Credit, and advisory on GST impact.

  • GSTR-1 / GSTR-3B support
  • GST reconciliation reports
  • Compliance checklists

Payroll & Salaries

Payroll processing, salary computation, PF & ESI calculations, and payslip generation.

  • Monthly payroll runs
  • TDS & statutory deductions
  • Payslip and salary register

Financial Reporting

Accurate monthly and annual reports, balance sheet, profit & loss, and cashflow analysis prepared for decision-making.

  • Monthly financial statements
  • Management reports & dashboards
  • Budgeting assistance

Tax-Ready Services

We prepare books in a tax-ready format so filing is smooth and audit risk is minimized.

  • Income tax computation support
  • Assistance during audits
  • Statutory compliance checks

Advisory & Cleanup

One-time cleanups for messy books, system setup, and advisory for accounting workflows and software selection.

  • Accounting system setup
  • Cleanup & error rectification
  • Software migration advice
